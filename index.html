📖 Programme Descriptions & Benefits

```

1. 💪 Functional Strength, Cardio & Cognitive Training

This programme focuses on improving overall physical function through a combination of strength training, cardiovascular conditioning, and cognitive reaction exercises. Participants will train using HUR resistance machines, suspension trainers, treadmills, dumbbells, resistance bands, battle ropes, boxing light reaction systems, and bodyweight movements. The exercises are designed to simulate real-life daily movements, helping participants build strength, coordination, endurance, and mental alertness in a safe and structured environment.

Benefits

🦵Builds muscle strength — Stand, walk, lift and stay independent.
🦴Strengthens bones & joints — Reduces risk of osteoporosis and joint degeneration.
🧍Improves posture & alignment — Corrects back, shoulder and knee issues.
❤️Enhances cardiovascular health — Better heart health, endurance and circulation.
🧠Boosts cognitive function — Reaction training improves focus and mental sharpness.
⚖️Improves balance & coordination — Reduces fall risk and builds confidence.
🛡️Reduces injury risk — Stronger muscles protect from strain and injuries.
🌟Promotes active aging — Encourages mobility, independence and well-being.

2. 🧘 Stretch & Strength

This programme focuses on gentle strength building combined with flexibility and mobility exercises to support healthy aging. Participants will use HUR machines, Pilates balls, chairs, resistance bands, light dumbbells, and bodyweight balance exercises to improve muscle strength, joint mobility, and overall body stability. The exercises are designed to be safe, controlled, and suitable for different fitness levels, making them ideal for individuals who want to stay active while improving flexibility and movement confidence.

Benefits

🚶Walk longer with less fatigue — Improved endurance for longer, comfortable walks.
🪜Climb stairs more easily — Stronger legs improve stair climbing and standing up.
🛍️Carry items with ease — Stronger arms and shoulders for daily tasks.
🤸Flexibility & joint mobility — Reduces stiffness and improves range of motion.
⚖️Balance & stability — Reduces slips and falls.
🧍Posture & spinal health — Reduces back discomfort and improves alignment.
🩸Improves circulation — Promotes blood flow and reduces muscle tension.
💪Boosts confidence — Feel more secure and independent in daily activities.
